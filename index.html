About Elizabeth

Therapy grounded in
warmth and rigour

I’m Elizabeth, a BACP registered psychotherapeutic counsellor. I work in an integrative way, grounded in transactional analysis, and my practice is trauma-informed. In plain terms, that means I pay attention to how the past shapes the present, and I won’t rush you somewhere you’re not ready to go.

Much of my work has been alongside people carrying something heavy, often on their own, and those who’ve spent so long looking after everyone else that they’ve lost sight of themselves. I listen properly, and the difficult things don’t frighten me.

You bring yourself, however you turn up that day. I bring curiosity, care and the training to help make sense of it. The rest we work out together.

Anxiety & Stress

When worry runs in the background all the time, it’s exhausting. Together we look at what’s driving it, and how to loosen its grip.

Women’s Health & Wellbeing

Living in a body that
asks a lot of you.
